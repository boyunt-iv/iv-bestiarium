This Privacy Policy applies to the website, OAuth connection flow, internal publishing tools, and related services operated for IV Bestiarium: The Hidden Ark (collectively, the "Service"). Depending on how the Service is used, we may process the following categories of information:

  • Publishing information: content files, captions, titles, descriptions, hashtags, scheduling instructions, post identifiers, upload status, platform response messages, and error logs related to publishing activity.

We use information only for purposes connected to operating and improving the Service, including:

  • detecting, investigating, and resolving technical errors, failed uploads, or API responses;
  • protecting the Service against unauthorized access, abuse, fraud, or misuse;
  • responding to privacy, legal, and support requests;
  • complying with applicable laws, platform policies, and developer obligations.

We do not use TikTok data to profile users, build advertising audiences, train artificial intelligence models, sell information, or share data with data brokers.

4. TikTok API Data and Limited Use

When a user authorizes TikTok access, the Service requests only the permissions needed for the intended publishing workflow.
